About BonAppMobile

BonAppMobile is a VR game where you're driving through different small villages in your pizza van, where you make... pizza. The driving happens automaticly. the player only focusses on making the pizza, steering by putting ingredients on the weel and delivering the pizza by throwing the pizza to the houses like a frizbee.

About Project

This is a VR project made by a bigger group of students (17 students) from the Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht and Media Collage Amsterdam. The goal of the project was to learn how to work in a smaller team (yet bigger than the teams we normally have at school) using scrum. We also learned Unreal Engine instead of using unity like we normally do at school. I was one of the developers on this project and was mainly focused on the oven and baking pizza. The project was about 10 weeks long
