Christmas Arena

A third person survival game!

About Christmas Arena

In Christmas Arena you have 2 players. The goal of the player is to kill the other player, but you are also being chased by snowman which throws snawballs at you.

About Project

This is an old small school project of like 2 or 3 weeks. Out team consisted of 3 developers. In this project, I was responisble for the map. We were provided with some assets, wich we were allowed to use in the game. As for development work, I didn't do al that much, but for some trouble shooting and fixen bugs when the other devs couldn't fix them. But in the end, I did make the entire map.
